Puntabelle Resort

Bataan

Puntabelle Resort




Do you know? Punta Belle Nature Resort has remained the most favourite place among nature lovers and for those loving the outdoors since 2010. This resort shares a creek bordering it, and swimming pools contain cool spring water. If you desire a location to beat the summer heat and get really close to nature, then this will be the perfect location. The place has multiple swimming pools; therefore, it is quite fascinating for not only the children to swim in and around but even for the adults.
You will have six swimming pools at the resort, designed both for kids and adults. There are two covered pools only for little ones and a big open pool in the north part of the resort. And yes, there are also exciting water slides for the kids to play on.
Note that the deepest pool cannot be used for diving, and it needs to be tidied up a little on your visit.

Accommodations: You get the cottages, nipa huts, and function room cottages within the proximity of pools and creek-it's pretty nice. Nipa huts, cabanas, and the different function rooms are likewise available.

Activity Area: The resort has an attractive lawn as perfect for different parties, team building and corporate events. Moreover, there's a volleyball court for little activities.

Mini Hiking: Going to the north side of the resort, you can view a garden with a super-sized chair and, on the other side of it, is a grotto where there's also a super-sized cross - two excellent spots for some really cool, nice Instagram pictures!

Restaurant and Souvenir Shop: Upon entering the Punta Belle resort, a restaurant and souvenir shop stand there right ahead. Longsilog foodstall was the only one available during our visit. No one is there at the souvenir shop.

Vegetable Garden Visit While roaming, we stumbled upon a vegetable garden. Whether it's open for visitation or not, you could always ask the staff. Just walk across the wood bridge situated behind the rooms and you might even get to pick some fresh produce there!
